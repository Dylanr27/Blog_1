Elden Ring Countdown

Nothing I'm More Excited For

02/09/2022

As a souls-type connoisueur, any game released in that genre generally piques my interest, but when FromSoftware releases a title, my excitment goes through the roof. The parents of the Souls games release nothing but pure greatness in my opinion. From the gut wrenching difficulty, to the simple yet complex combat to the amazing level design to the incredible rpg mechanics, it has the whole 9 yards and it is more than safe to say the souls games are my favorite. 16 days to go.

When Two Worlds Collide

02/11/2022

FromSoftware has enlisted Game Of Thrones author George R.R Martin, to concieve the lore and world that Elden Ring will take place in. With an incredibly imaginative mind the likes of Martin, collaborating with renowed studio Director Hidetaka Miyazaki, one can only image the crazy things we will see in the world of Elden Ring. In my estimation, the title is set to be FromSoftwares most amazing game yet. 14 days to go.
