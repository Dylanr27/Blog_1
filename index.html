About me:

I have been a gamer all my life, video games are my first love and they will always be a major part of my life. That being said more than anything I want to create a video game of my own. I play all genres of games, such as RTS, deck builders, rougelikes, horror games, zen games, souls, rpg's, fps, etc, I play them all. I have always dreamed of being a part of the development of video games, and I hope this project will be the first of many to come.
